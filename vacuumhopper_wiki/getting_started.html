Getting Started

Vacuum Hopper

To create a vacuum hopper, you will need an eye of ender, a piece of obsidian, and one hopper.

Extras

To go along with your shiny new vacuum hopper, you may want a vacuum screwdriver and/or a vacuum filter.

A vacuum screwdriver allows you to edit the inputs and outputs of your vacuum hopper.

A vacuum filter allows you to blacklist and whitelist items to pickup in the vacuum hopper.

Vacuum Screwdriver

To create a vacuum screwdriver, you will need a piece of lapis, a gold ingot, and an iron ingot.

Vacuum Filter

To create a vacuum filter, you will need 4 planks, 2 pieces of string, a comparator, and a piece of redstone.
